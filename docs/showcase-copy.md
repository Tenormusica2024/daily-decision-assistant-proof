## Work title

Daily Decision Assistant Proof

## Short description

A sample-first proof that turns mixed daily signals into three groups: focus today, defer, and no-go. Items that would require an external action are not executed automatically; they are routed into a confirmation queue for human review.

## Safety note

This proof runs only on synthetic sample data. It does not connect to email, calendar, task-management, CRM, or other external services. It demonstrates decision support and confirmation queueing only.

## Do not overclaim

- Do not describe it as an autonomous executor.
- Do not imply live calendar or email integration.
- Do not imply it uses private company data.
- Do not present it as a production task manager.
